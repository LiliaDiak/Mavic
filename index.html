What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel perfect for aerial photography. The drone has all the best DJI technology, it will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c features Hasselblad's unique Natural Color Solution (HNCS)5 technology, enabling users to capture stunning aerial shots with 20 megapixel resolution and stunning colors.
